Things Cat Lovers Enjoy:

  • Cats: They are known for their playful and independent nature.
  • Laser pointers: Cats love to chase the light from laser pointers.
  • Catnip: This herb makes many cats playful and energetic.

Cats love lasagna, a dish often enjoyed by humans.

Top 3 Things Cats Hate:

  1. Flea problems: Fleas can cause discomfort and itching in cats.
  2. Thunder: Many cats are frightened by loud thunder during storms.
  3. Other cats: Some cats prefer being solitary and may not get along with others.

Cats dislike sharing their space with other cats.
